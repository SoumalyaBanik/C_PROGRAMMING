TheyWe - Women Safety Platform

A secure platform to ensure your safety by connecting with volunteers and authorities.

How to Use?

  1. Volunteers should register through Volunteer Signup.
  2. Users should register through User Signup.
  3. Once registered, login by entering your username and password.
  4. After logging in, the service will start. In case of an emergency, press the panic button for immediate assistance.
  5. You can also notify volunteers about suspicious activity through the Notify section.
  6. Our platform ensures your data's security through encryption and salting, safeguarding your information and safety.

About the Platform

This platform allows women in danger to connect with nearby volunteers and police instantly using GPS. We aim to create a safer environment through community support and technology.
